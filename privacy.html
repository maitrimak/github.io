Volunteer Connect

Privacy Statement

1. Information Collection, Use, and Disclosure

We collect personal information when you register online, such as your name, email address, and phone number. We use this information to provide you with updates about volunteering opportunities and other services related to Volunteer Connect.

2. Protection of Your Personal Information

Your personal information will be protected by security safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information. We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization unless permitted or required by law. When we do share your information, we will limit disclosure in accordance with the purposes identified.

3. Contact Information

If you supply us with your contact information, you may hear from us about important information related to your interest in volunteering. If you do not wish to receive information about other Volunteer Connect programs, services, or opportunities, please email us.

4. Links on Our Website

Because Volunteer Connect wants your website experience to be informational and resourceful, we also provide a number of links to third-party sites. Volunteer Connect assumes no responsibility for the information practices of sites you are able to access through our site. We encourage you to review each site's privacy policy before disclosing any personally identifiable information.

5. Your Access to and Control Over Information

Volunteer Connect strives to ensure that your personal information is accurate, complete, and up-to-date as necessary for the purposes for which it is to be used. You have the right to access, update, and correct your personal information by contacting us at the provided email address.
